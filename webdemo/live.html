Freight carrier due diligence

Know who you’re hiring, before the load and after.

Since Montgomery v. Caribe Transport (May 2026), a freight broker can be sued for the carrier they pick. CarrierGuard vets any carrier against live FMCSA data and keeps watching it for as long as it hauls.

Type any US carrier’s MC number, or try one of these:

  • Pulls that carrier’s live record from the FMCSA QCMobile API
  • Scores it with a fixed, versioned policy, not the model
  • Writes a dated record to an append-only audit log
